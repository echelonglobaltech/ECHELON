Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

At ECHELON, we understand that confidentiality is the cornerstone of our service. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and fiercely protect your personal and academic information.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information strictly necessary to deliver our engineering services:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, and phone number provided via our inquiry form.
  • Project Specifications: University guidelines, grading rubrics, project briefs, and technical requirements.
  • Technical Data: Standard server logs, IP addresses, and browser data for security and performance monitoring.

2. Our Strict NDA & Confidentiality Guarantee

We operate under an implicit Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) for every client. We will never:

  • Share your identity, university, or project details with any third party, academic institution, or external agency.
  • Reuse, resell, or publish your custom-built codebase for other clients.
  • Claim public credit for the work we develop for you.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Architecting and delivering your academic project.
  • Communicating with you regarding milestones, reviews, and post-delivery support.
  • Processing milestone payments securely.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Upon successful defense and completion of your 30-day/60-day post-delivery support period, we purge all identifiable project data, databases, and source code from our active development servers. You retain full ownership of the final deliverables.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about our privacy practices or wish to request immediate data deletion, please contact us at: echelonglobaltech@gmail.com.
